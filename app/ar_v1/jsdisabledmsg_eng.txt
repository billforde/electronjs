<h3>In-Document Analytics Requires JavaScript</h3>

<p>
To open In-Document Analytics content, JavaScript needs to be enabled in your web browser.
</p>

<p>
On mobile devices, please use the Mobile Faves App. If not installed download it from the 
<a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/mobile-faves/id406033421?mt=8">App Store</a> for iOS devices or from the
<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ibi.android.mobilefaves&hl=en">Google Play Store</a>
for Android devices.
</p>
